#God be thanked A sermon of thanksgiuing for the happy successe of the English fleetes, sent forth by the honourable company of aduenturers to the East Indies. Preached to the honourable gouernors and committees, and the whole company, of their good ship, the Hope Marchant happily returened: at Deptford on Maundy Thursday last being the 29th of March. 1616. Hereunto are added sundry necessary and vseful formes of prayer and thankes-giuing for the helpe of all such as trauell by sea, fitted to their seruerall occasions. By Samuel Page Dr. in Diuinitie.#

1. TO THE RIGHT
WORSHIPFVLL THE
Maſter, Wardens, and Aſsiſtants,
and the whole Societie of the Brethren
of the Trinitie-houſe, of Saint
CLEMENTS in Deptford-Strand.

1. Diuine Sea-ſeruice,
Containing ſundry neceſſary and vſefull
formes of prayer and thankes-giuing, for
the helpe of all ſuch as trauaile by ſea, fitted
to their ſeuerall neceſsities.

    _ A forme of Prayer to be vſed in our owne land
for all our brethren at Sea.

    _ A forme of Prayer to be vſed at the
ſetting forth of any ſhippes in
any voyage to Sea.

    _ A Prayer to be vſed at Sea
for our owne Land.

    _ A Morning Prayer.

    _ A Prayer before Meate.

    _ A thankes-giuing after meat.

    _ An Euening Prayer.

    _ A Prayer to bee vſed in a time of
danger, by Stormes, and Tempeſts
at Sea.

    _ A Thankeſ-giuing for deliuerance
from the former danger of tempeſt
and foule weather.

    _ A Prayer in danger of the
enemie.

    _ A Thankeſ-giuing for deliuerance
from the former danger of the
enemie.

    _ A Prayer to bee vſed aboard in any
viſitation, by infectious ſickneſſe.

    _ A Thankeſ-giuing for deliuerance
from infectious ſickeneſſe.

    _ A Prayer for their friends at home,
to be vſed by them who aduenture
their owne perſons to Sea.

    _ A Prayer to bee vſed aboard, for
the Aduenturers and Owners at
home.

    _ A thankes-giuing for a faire Wind.
